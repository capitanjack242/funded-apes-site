EU / ESMA Risk Disclosure

Under European Securities and Markets Authority (ESMA) regulations, leveraged trading products such as CFDs, futures, and margin forex are considered complex financial instruments and are not suitable for all investors. Retail clients face a high risk of losing money rapidly due to leverage.
